Reconstruction of the urban market in Pleszew

In the project concerning the reconstruction of the urban market in Pleszew, an innovative concept has been designed, which is based on the use of a modular structure for the square's flooring. This project was developed taking into account the existing market grid, allowing for the introduction of modular small architectural objects.

The new structures not only provide space for relaxation and leisure but also areas dedicated to greenery and gastronomy. By employing a modular design, it is possible to flexibly adapt these structures to different functions and the needs of the local community. To counter the dominance of concrete, it was decided to replace a portion of the flooring with greenery. This initiative also enables effective management of rainwater, which accumulates throughout the market space. Through appropriate rainwater management, it is possible to minimize the consequences related to excessive flooding or runoff, thus contributing to the improvement of the urban environment's quality.

The reconstruction of the urban market in Pleszew, based on the designed concept, has increased the attractiveness of this location by offering residents and visitors diverse opportunities for relaxation, enjoying green spaces, and indulging in gastronomic offerings.
